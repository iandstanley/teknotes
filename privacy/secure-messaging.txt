SECURE MESSAGING
================

matrix.org

	client = element.io (available for all platforms)
	you can also run your own server

AVOID TELEGRAM AND WHATSAPP

	You should avoid telegram and whatsapp as they are 
	not end-2-end encrypted.

signal.org

	not open source but it is e2e encrypted

disroot.org

	with only a handful of users they shutdown their 
	Matrix server because it had grown to 5GB ram and 
	170gb HDD. They switched to XMPP

xmpp.org

	a privacy respecting, opensource platform since 1999
	xmpp has a wider choice of clients

briar messaging

	Android / F-Droid only. Highly secure and doesn't 
	require internet!
